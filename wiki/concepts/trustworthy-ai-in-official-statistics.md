In this corpus, trustworthy AI refers to AI use that remains compatible with statistical quality, institutional accountability, privacy protection, and public trust.

## Grounded dimensions

- validation of AI-based methods
- transparency and explainability expectations
- privacy and confidentiality protection
- adaptation of quality frameworks
- institutional skills and governance capacity

## Important note

The corpus does not reduce trustworthy AI to explainability alone. It is a broader methodological and institutional operating principle.
