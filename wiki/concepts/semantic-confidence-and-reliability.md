The corpus repeatedly separates semantic retrieval performance from the question of whether a prediction is reliable enough to use.

## Grounded components

- ranking-margin-based semantic confidence
- confidence thresholds
- prediction sets
- uncertainty quantification
- conformal calibration in the ATECO chapter

## Synthesized role

These mechanisms are used as bridges between AI output and statistical decision-making. They enable selective automation, targeted review, and stronger methodological accountability.
