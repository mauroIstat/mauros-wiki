The scientific material is compact, claim-bounded, and repeatedly distinguishes methodological contribution from broader implication.

## Recurrent features

- explicit problem framing
- named methods and datasets
- reported performance or validation claims
- cautious interpretation of evidence
- limitations around ground truth, transferability, or operational use
