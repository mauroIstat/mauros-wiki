The teaching material prioritizes intuition, contrast, and audience engagement over exhaustive formal detail.

## Recurrent features

- concrete examples before abstraction
- contrastive framing such as rules vs learning or words vs meaning
- rhetorical questions and prompts
- compressed takeaways at the end of sections
- careful simplification without dropping the reliability caveat
