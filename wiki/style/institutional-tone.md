The institutional material presents AI as a strategic capability that must remain aligned with public value, statistical quality, and responsible governance.

## Recurrent features

- modernization and accessibility framing
- emphasis on public trust
- explicit reference to privacy, transparency, and regulation
- practical rather than promotional language
- stress on skills, research, and institutional coordination
