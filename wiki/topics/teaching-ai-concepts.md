The teaching-oriented part of the corpus explains AI by translating technical mechanisms into intuitive contrasts, visual metaphors, and audience-facing questions.

## Recurrent explanatory moves

- rule-based systems vs learned representations
- lexical matching vs semantic matching
- isolated words vs context-dependent meaning
- raw data difficulty vs better representation

## Tone markers

- rhetorical questions
- concrete examples before abstraction
- compact definitions followed by consequences
- explicit audience guidance and discussion prompts
