PRODCOM classification appears as a concrete AI-supported workflow where company websites are used to extract product evidence and assign official product codes for business-survey support.

## Grounded elements

- The 2026 paper defines a staged pipeline: URL identification, product evidence extraction, and code assignment.
- The workflow is explicitly designed to support questionnaire pre-filling and coding validation.
- Alternative coding engines coexist within the same pipeline, including embedding-based retrieval, LLM-assisted classification, and supervised models.
- The use case reinforces a recurring corpus idea: AI acts as an assistive tool for statistical offices rather than a purely autonomous classifier.
