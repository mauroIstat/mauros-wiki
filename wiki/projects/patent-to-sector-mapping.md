Patent-to-sector mapping is a methodological project that uses semantic retrieval to connect invention descriptions to economic categories and studies how ranking structure can support reliability assessment.

## Grounded elements

- Patents are treated as rich technological text that does not naturally map to economic sectors.
- The paper studies ranking behavior and semantic confidence.
- Workshop material reuses patents as a representative non-traditional source for official-statistics AI.
- The neighboring 2025 occupational-exposure paper shows a related patent-based semantic pipeline applied to occupations rather than sectors.
