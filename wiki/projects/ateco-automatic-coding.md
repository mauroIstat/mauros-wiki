ATECO automatic coding is the clearest recurring applied project in the corpus: map free-text descriptions of economic activity to official codes using semantic retrieval while preserving statistical defensibility.

## Grounded elements

- ATECO is used as a concrete case for automatic coding.
- The chapter evaluates alternative knowledge-base designs and reranking.
- The workshop material contrasts deterministic CIRCE-style logic with embedding-based approaches.
- Validation plans include baseline comparison, human-labeled evidence, and later model specialization.

## Recurring methodological stance

- Better semantic retrieval improves candidate generation.
- Operational use still requires uncertainty handling and expert validation.
